The Coyoneda Lemmaüîó

The Coyoneda lemma is, like its dual, a statement about presheaves. It states that ‚Äúevery presheaf is a colimit of representables,‚Äù which, in less abstract terms, means that every presheaf arises as some way of gluing together a bunch of (things isomorphic to) hom functors!

We start by fixing some presheaf PPP, and constructing a Cocone whose coapex is PPP. This involves a clever choice of diagram category: specifically, the category of elements of PPP.
